Meals For Hope

The MOBI is Zimbabwe's very first Mobi Soup Kitchen, providing nutritious meals and support to women and children in some of our most vulnerable nd insolated communities.

The MOBI was launched in October 2021 and is already providing its beneficiaries with cruial access to nutrition, support, education, healthcare and long term change.

The MOBI is an intiative that aims to reduce hunger and poverty in women and children, with a focus:
  • Food & nutrition
  • Health and psychosocial support
  • Education and literacy
  • Disaster/emergency response and relief
  • Awareness, fundraising and support of homeless artisans
